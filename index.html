About us

KoreX Energy is a renewable energy company focused on transforming Nigeria’s vast agricultural and natural resources into high-value, low-carbon bioethanol for local use and global export. Through efficient hydrolysis, fermentation, and purification processes, we deliver export-grade bioethanol that meets international standards while reducing environmental impact. Our mission is to bridge the gap between Nigeria’s untapped potential and its current energy position by building sustainable industrial infrastructure, strengthening local supply chains, and enabling cleaner energy solutions that drive long-term economic and environmental progress.
